## üöÄ Quick Start

1. **Open the application** - Simply open `index.html` in your web browser
2. **Paste your AI text** - Input your AI-generated content in the left text area
3. **Select writing style** - Choose from Academic, Casual, Creative, or Professional
4. **Configure options** - Adjust humanization level and error injection settings
5. **Humanize** - Click the "Humanize Text" button and watch the transformation
6. **Test detection** - Use the built-in AI detector testing panel (Ctrl/Cmd + T)
7. **Export results** - Download your humanized text in your preferred format
